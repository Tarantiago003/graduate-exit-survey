Why We're Asking

Purpose: To gather meaningful feedback from graduates about every aspect of the graduation experience — from finance and registration to the ceremony itself. Your insights directly shape how MVGFC serves future graduates.

Data Use: Responses are analyzed solely for institutional improvement — to strengthen communication, address logistics, and improve support for graduates and their families. All data is kept confidential.
